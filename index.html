Private hiking journal for iPhone

Turn hikes into lasting memories.

Wanderbuch helps you create a personal hiking journal with routes, photos, statistics, and places discovered along the way — from quick forest walks to full mountain ascents.

Everything around your hike, in one calm place.

Wanderbuch is built for personal memories, detailed routes, and quiet reflection — not accounts, ads, or social pressure.

01

Apple Health workouts

View hiking workouts from Apple Health and create personal hike logs from them.

02

Interactive route maps

Explore your route with detailed workout statistics, photos, videos, climbs, and descents.

03

Photos along your route

See photos and videos connected to where they were captured during your hike.

04

Nearby hiking spots

Discover nearby peaks, mountains, viewpoints, and hiking spots around your route.

05

Monthly and yearly statistics

Follow distance, elevation, hikes, and places through calm hiking summaries.

06

Journal-style overview

Add custom cover photos and revisit each hike as a personal memory, not just a workout.
