# Wanderbuch

Wanderbuch helps you turn hikes into lasting memories.

Create a personal hiking journal filled with routes, photos, statistics, and places you discovered along the way.

After finishing a hike, Wanderbuch can detect your hiking workouts from Apple Health and help you turn them into detailed hike memories with maps, media, and rich workout insights.

Whether it is a short forest walk or a full mountain ascent, Wanderbuch helps you relive every adventure.

## Features

* Turn Apple Health workouts into hike memories
* Interactive route maps with detailed workout statistics
* Photos and videos displayed along your route
* Discover nearby peaks, mountains, and hiking spots
* Highlight steep climbs and descents
* Monthly and yearly hiking statistics
* Custom hike cover photos
* Full-screen map exploration
* Journal-style hike overview
* Explore previously visited and nearby hiking spots

Wanderbuch is built to feel personal, calm, and private.

No accounts. No ads. No tracking.
Your hiking memories stay on your device.
